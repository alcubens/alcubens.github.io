Biography

Rybakova Tetiana

In 1995 graduated with honors from Alchevsk secondary school No. 1 with in-depth study of the English language. In 1995, she entered full-time study at the Donbass Mining and Metallurgical Institute (now Donbass State Technical University), Alchevsk. In 1996, she entered extramural studies at the Gorlovka State Pedagogical Institute of Foreign Languages, Gorlovka. From 1995 to 2001 she studied full-time in the specialty "Organization Management" at the Donbass Mining and Metallurgical Institute (now Donbass State Technical University), Alchevsk. In 1999, she received a bachelor’s qualification, in 2000, a specialist’s qualification, and in 2001, a master’s qualification in the specialty “Organization Management”.
In 2001 she graduated from the Gorlovka State Pedagogical Institute of Foreign Languages, received the qualification of a specialist, specialty "English teacher".

Present days

While working at the Department of Management of Foreign Economic Activities, she performed the duties of the head of the practice of students of the specialty "Management of Foreign Economic Activities" and the scientific secretary of the department. I read the following disciplines: 
- international economic relations;
- commodity nomenclature of foreign trade activities;
- international Management;
- management of foreign economic activity;
- international economic organizations;
- foreign exchange regulation of foreign trade activities;
- payment systems.
